Beans Recipe

This is a recipe for simple boiled beans. The bare minimum.

Ingredients

  • Beans
  • Water
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Wash beans twice.
  2. Fill a pot with water. Add your beans to the pot.
  3. Add salt.
  4. Bring water to a boil.
  5. Cook until beans are tender.
  6. Serve in a bowl.
