Sauteed Chicken Breast Recipe

This is a recipe for a simple sauteed chicken breast.

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast
  • Oil
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Add oil to a pan and heat it.
  2. Once the pan is hot enough, place your chicken breast in the pan and let it cook 5 minutes each side.
  3. Add salt.
  4. Once both sides are cooked and golden, take it out of the pan.
  5. Serve in a plate.
